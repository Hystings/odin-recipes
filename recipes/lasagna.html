Lasagna

Description:

Layered cheese, mince sauce and pasta


Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Mince
  • Dolmio's Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese Sauce

Method:

Make Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in frying pan
  2. Add mince to frying pan, Breaking up with spoon, cook until golden brown
  3. Add Dolmi's Tomato Sauce
  4. Cook until slightly thick

Layer Lasagne

  1. Add a layer of pasta to a casarole dish
  2. Add a layer of mince sauce
  3. Add a layer of cheese sauce
  4. Add a layer of pasta
  5. Add a layer of mince sauce
  6. Add a layer of cheese sauce
  7. Add a layer of pasta
  8. Add a layer of cheese sauce

Cook in oven at 210c for 30 minutes.
