Tomato Soup

Description:

A wholesome tomato soup


Ingredients:

  • 2x 500g Tinned Tomatos
  • 2x Chicken Stock Cubes
  • 2x Brown Onions
  • 1x tbsp Minced Garlic
  • Drizzle of Olive Oil
  • 1x tbsp Italian Herbs
  • 1x tbsp Paprika

Method:

  1. Heat Olive Oil in Large pot
  2. Chop Onions
  3. Cook onions until soft, 2-3 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, boil 1 litre of water in kettle.
  5. Add Garlic, Italian Herbs and Paprika, cooking until fragrant, 1 minute.
  6. Add tinned Tomatos, Water and Stock Cubes.
  7. Cook on High, 20-30 minutes.
  8. Optionally, Blend with hand blender
